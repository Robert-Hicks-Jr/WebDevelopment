Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create a resume using the tool?

To create a resume, simply choose a template, customize it with your information, and then export it in your preferred format (PDF, Word, etc.).

2. Is the resume builder free to use?

Yes, the basic version of our resume builder is completely free. However, we also offer premium features for users who want additional customization options.

3. Can I save my resume and come back to edit it later?

Yes, you can save your progress securely in the cloud and return to edit your resume at any time from any device.

4. Are there any templates for different job industries?

Yes, we offer a variety of templates tailored for different job industries, including technology, education, marketing, and more.

5. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team by emailing info@resumebuilder.com. We are happy to assist with any questions you may have!

6. Can I download my resume in multiple formats?

Yes, you can download your resume in various formats, including PDF, Word, and more.

7. How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, please contact our support team at info@resumebuilder.com and request account deletion. We'll assist you with the process.
